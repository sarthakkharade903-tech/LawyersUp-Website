# LawyersUp

An AI-powered legal assistant that helps users draft legal complaints, identify complaint categories, generate legal documents, and understand the next steps they can take.

## Problem

Many people do not know how to write a legal complaint or determine which type of complaint applies to their situation. Legal procedures can feel confusing and intimidating.

## Solution

LawyersUp simplifies legal assistance by using AI to generate structured complaints, classify legal issues, provide document templates, support multiple languages, export PDFs, and help users track their cases through a user-friendly interface.

## Features

- AI Complaint Generation
- Complaint Category Detection
- Multi-Language Support
- PDF Complaint Export
- Legal Document Templates
- Case Tracking System
- User-Friendly Interface
- Instant AI Responses
